Abstract

 
             Petrol and diesel cars contribute a lot to global warming, so a solution must be made to reduce to effect of global warming. 
             Electric cars offer one solution, but it may not be the best, this paper aims to investigate to pros and cons of hydrogen 
             powered and electric powered cars and how this could benefit the Earth. As you can probably see, nearly all car manufacturers 
             are focused on electric cars rather than hydrogen, this is partly because of the risks of hydrogen power and the cost of 
             developing the necessary technology to make hydrogen power work. This would indicate that right now at least, electric cars 
             are the best alternative over the petrol and diesel cars that dominate the market.
